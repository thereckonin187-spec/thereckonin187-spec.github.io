What we make

Vault Printables designs and sells functional printable PDFs — planners, checklists, study guides and worksheets — built for specific audiences (nurses, tradespeople, notaries, and more). Each product is an instant digital download.

About this application

Our internal application helps us run the business: it prepares product files and publishes short promotional videos to our own YouTube channel and social accounts. It uses the YouTube Data API only to upload videos to the channel we own and operate. It does not read, collect, or access any other YouTube user's data.
